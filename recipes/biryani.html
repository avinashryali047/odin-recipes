Vegetable Biryani

This Hyderabadi Veg Biryani is an authentic Indian vegetarian recipe packed full of your favorite rice, veggies, and spices. Made with
patience and lots of love, this delicious veg dum biryani requires both care and attention to detail. The savory flavors in your first bite
are well worth the time and effort spent in the kitchen.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Water
  • Veggies
  • Dairy
  • Nuts
  • Seasonings

Steps

  1. Soak the Rice
  2. Prep the Veggies
  3. Cook the Rice
  4. Add Spices to Boiling Water
  5. Add Rice
  6. Make Biryani Vegetable Gravy
  7. Add Yogurt or Curd
  8. Cook the Vegetables
  9. Assemble and Layer Vegetable Biryani
