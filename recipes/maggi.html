Maggi

Maggi noodles are dried noodles fused with oil, and sold with a packet of flavorings. These noodles are usually eaten after being cooked
in boiling water for 3 to 5 minutes or eaten straight from the packet

Ingredients

  • 140 gm maggi noodles
  • 2 1/2 cup water
  • 1 teaspoon butter
  • 1 tomato, 1 green chilli, 1/2 onion, 1/4 cup peas, 1/2 capsicum (green pepper)
  • 12 gm maggi masala
  • 1/4 teaspoon garam masala powder, 1/2 teaspoon garlic paste, 1/4 teaspoon powdered turmeric, 1/4 teaspoon powdered black pepper,
    1/4 tablespoon red chilli powder, 1/2 teaspoon chaat masala powder, salt as required

Steps

  1. Boil water & chop the veggies
  2. Cook Maggi and saute veggies in a separate pan
  3. Add spices
  4. Garnish and enjoy
